# Pipeline Review — repo-dd — 2026-06-05

## Summary

`/repo-dd` is the workspace due-diligence orchestrator: a three-tier pipeline (factual audit → deep operational assessment → pipeline testing) that delegates the heavy reads to three subagents and keeps the main session as a thin coordinator with two operator gates. Its design center is correct and load-bearing — evidence/interpretation separation across separate report files (DR-6, QS-4), independent factual audit in fresh subagent context (QS-1), and a Subagent Contracts posture where the main session reads extracts not raw reports (ai-resources/CLAUDE.md § Subagent Contracts). The pipeline is structurally sound at this read: all three subagents resolve, frontmatter tiers are coherent (auditor `sonnet`, two extractors `haiku`), and the recent commit history shows active maintenance of stale paths. The shape risk it carries today is drift-of-omission rather than breakage: the command frontmatter predates the unified skills/commands convention, and two reserved-but-empty step slots and a heavy step-numbering scheme add reading cost without function. No Blocking or Substantive brokenness found. The currency check passed with three Minor convention divergences against the pinned platform doc.

## Innovations proposed

- **Add a `triage-reviewer` pass on AUTO-FIX classification** — Step 4 self-classifies findings into AUTO-FIX/OPERATOR/INFO inside the main session, which is exactly the self-evaluation QS-1 warns against; routing the classification through the existing `triage-reviewer` agent would give the categorization the same independence the factual audit already has (QS-2: QC findings go through triage before action). Affects: `ai-resources/.claude/commands/repo-dd.md` (Step 4), reuses `triage-reviewer` agent. Risk class: new canonical-command-edit class — `/risk-check` gated (risk-topology.md § 3).
- **Fold pipeline-test preconditions into the deep report's untracked-dependency check** — Step 13's five precondition tests (symlink resolution, template sync, deploy/new-project/sync preconditions) overlap conceptually with Step 9's untracked-dependency tracing; surfacing the test failures as Critical findings in Section 1.3 rather than a separate Section 4 would consolidate the operational-dependency story in one place. Affects: `repo-dd.md` Steps 13, 56. Risk class: minor (report-structure only, no shared-state effect).
- **Declare the manual-invoke contract in frontmatter** — `/repo-dd` writes files and commits; the platform now documents `disable-model-invocation: true` as the convention for side-effecting workflows the operator triggers manually (currency check below). Declaring it makes the pipeline's manual-only posture explicit and prevents Claude auto-firing a commit-bearing audit. Affects: `repo-dd.md` frontmatter. Risk class: minor.

## Leanness fixes

- **Remove the two reserved-empty step slots (Steps 50, 51)** — both read "(Reserved — log discovery and reading are now performed by the sweep agent in Step 34.)"; the work moved to `dd-log-sweep-agent` but the numbered placeholders remain, costing read tokens on every invocation and implying a step exists where none does (AP-10 — defensive structure for a state that cannot occur). Estimated token reduction: small. Risk class: minor.
- **Collapse the dual numbering scheme** — the body carries both H3 step headings (Step 1–14) and an independent running ordinal (1–71); the two diverge (e.g., "Step 4" contains items 14–16), which forces the reader to hold two counters. Dropping the running ordinal in favor of the H3 step numbers would lighten the body. Estimated token reduction: small-to-moderate. Risk class: minor — but verify no downstream command or doc cites the ordinal numbers as a two-end contract before editing (risk-topology.md § 5 signal: string literals matched by another component).
- **Lift the repeated "Do not push" / commit-format prose into a single reference** — commit-message format and "Do NOT push" appear in both Step 7 and Step 14 with near-identical wording; the canonical commit/push rules already live in ai-resources/CLAUDE.md, so the command could cite rather than restate. Estimated token reduction: small. Risk class: minor.
